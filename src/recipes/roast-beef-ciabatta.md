## Ingredients

- 4 slices roast beef
- 1 ciabatta bun
- White cheddar cheese, sliced
- Greens (lettuce or arugula)
- Red onion, thinly sliced
- Horseradish
- Garlic mayo
- Pesto
- Butter

## Instructions

1. Defrost the roast beef if frozen.
2. Butter the cut sides of the ciabatta and toast in a pan until golden.
3. Slice the cheese, red onion, and prep the greens.
4. Spread garlic mayo on one side, pesto on the other, and a thin swipe of horseradish to taste.
5. Layer on the roast beef, cheddar, red onion, and greens. Serve immediately.
