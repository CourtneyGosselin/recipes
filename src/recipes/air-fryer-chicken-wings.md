## Ingredients

- 1 package chicken wings
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp poultry seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp lemon pepper

## Instructions

1. Thaw wings in the microwave.
2. Combine all seasonings in a bowl or zip-lock bag. Add the wings and toss until fully coated.
3. Place wings in the air fryer and spritz lightly with oil to prevent seasoning from blowing up into the fan.
4. Air fry at 400°F for 27 minutes. Serve immediately.
