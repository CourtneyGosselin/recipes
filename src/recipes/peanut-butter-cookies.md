## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup granulated sugar, plus extra for flattening
- 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/4 cup shortening
- 1/4 cup salted butter, softened
- 1 large egg
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- M&Ms (as many as you like)

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, beat both sugars, peanut butter, shortening, butter, and egg on medium speed until smooth and thoroughly blended.
2. Stir in the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until just combined. Don't overmix — it makes the cookies tough.
3. Fold in the M&Ms.
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for about 2 hours until firm. The dough should be cold enough to roll into balls easily. If it's still too soft, chill another 30 minutes.
5. Heat oven to 375°F. Shape dough into 1¼-inch balls and place 3 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Keep the remaining dough in the fridge while each batch bakes.
6. Dip a fork in the extra granulated sugar and flatten each ball with a crisscross pattern. Re-dip the fork each time to prevent sticking.
7. Bake 9–10 minutes until light golden brown.
8. Cool 5 minutes on the sheet, then transfer to a wire rack. Cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
