## Ingredients

- Salmon, cut into cubes
- 4 tbsp teriyaki sauce, plus more to serve
- 2 sweet potatoes
- Butter
- Feta cheese, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Cut the salmon into cubes, place on an air fryer sheet, and coat with 4 tbsp teriyaki sauce.
2. Air fry at 400°F for 20 minutes.
3. While the salmon cooks, microwave the sweet potatoes for 8 minutes until tender.
4. Split open the sweet potatoes and add a dab of butter to each.
5. Plate the sweet potato and top with the salmon. Add more teriyaki sauce if needed and finish with a generous crumble of feta.
