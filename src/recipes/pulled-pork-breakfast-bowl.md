## Ingredients

- Pulled pork leftovers
- BBQ sauce
- Hashbrowns
- 2 eggs (soft yolk)
- Green onions, sliced
- Sesame seeds
- Aioli, for dipping

## Instructions

1. Get two pans going at the same time — cook the hashbrowns in one, and warm the pulled pork with a generous splash of BBQ sauce in the other.
2. Once the hashbrowns and pork are nearly done, fry the eggs to a soft yolk in whichever pan has space.
3. Plate starting with the hashbrowns, then pulled pork, then the egg on top. Finish with green onions, sesame seeds, and a drizzle of BBQ sauce.
4. Serve with aioli on the side for dipping.
