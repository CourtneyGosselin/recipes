## Ingredients

### BBQ Pulled Pork
- 4 lb boneless pork shoulder, fat trimmed
- 1 yellow onion, sliced
- 1–2 cups BBQ sauce
- Ginger ale (enough to cover in the Instant Pot)

### Mac and Cheese
- 1 lb elbow macaroni
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 cups whole milk, warmed
- 4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese

## Instructions

### Pulled Pork (Instant Pot)

1. Add pork shoulder, yellow onion, and ginger ale to your 6-quart Instant Pot.
2. Set to manual high pressure for 75 minutes.
3. Let the pressure release naturally — don't rush it.
4. Remove the pork to a large plate and shred with two forks, discarding any large chunks of fat.
5. Pour out the liquid from the pot, return the shredded pork, and stir in 1 cup BBQ sauce. Add more to taste — you want the meat coated but not swimming. Cover to keep warm.

### Mac and Cheese

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray a 9×13 casserole dish with non-stick spray.
2. Cook the macaroni in a large pot of salted boiling water until just al dente. Drain and set aside.
3. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Whisk in the flour and cook 1 minute.
4. Slowly whisk in the warm milk until smooth. Reduce heat to low and keep whisking until the sauce is thick and creamy.
5. Stir in the cheddar until fully melted. Turn off the heat and fold in the macaroni.
6. Pour into the prepared dish and top with pulled pork. Add a sprinkle of extra cheese if you like.
7. Bake for 15 minutes until warmed through. Drizzle with extra BBQ sauce before serving.

---

> You'll likely have leftover pulled pork — it keeps well and is great in the breakfast bowl.
